About this kit

The NHSBT Prototype Kit is based on the NHS.UK Prototype Kit, customised with NHSBT branding including:

  • NHSBT header with organisation logo and service name banner
  • NHSBT footer with social media links, additional links and related sites
  • NHSBT colour scheme (dark blue #003087 header)
  • All NHS Design System components available as Nunjucks macros
  • Automatic form data storage in session
  • Live reload during development
